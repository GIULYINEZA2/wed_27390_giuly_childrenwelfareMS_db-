# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

## 1. Child Welfare KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Target | Frequency |
|-----|---------|--------|-----------|
| Service Coverage | (Children served / Total children) × 100 | >95% | Monthly |
| Average Service Gap | AVG(Days between services) | <30 days | Weekly |
| Disability Support | (Disabled children with services / Total disabled) × 100 | 100% | Monthly |
| Education Enrollment | (Children in school / School-age children) × 100 | >90% | Quarterly |

## 2. Operational KPIs
| KPI | Formula | Target |
|-----|---------|--------|
| Staff Workload | (Services assigned / Staff count) | <20 services/week |
| Processing Time | Time from registration to first service | <48 hours |
| Data Accuracy | (Error-free records / Total records) × 100 | >99% |
| System Availability | (Uptime / Total time) × 100 | >99.5% |

## 3. Compliance KPIs
| KPI | Description | Target |
|-----|-------------|--------|
| Audit Compliance | % of operations properly logged | 100% |
| Rule Violations | Number of DML attempts blocked | 0 |
| Data Integrity | Foreign key constraint violations | 0 |
