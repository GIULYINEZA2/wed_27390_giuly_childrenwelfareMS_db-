# Business Intelligence Requirements

## Stakeholders
1. **Executive Management** - Strategic decisions, resource allocation
2. **Case Workers** - Daily operations, child monitoring
3. **Administrators** - System performance, compliance
4. **Donors/Partners** - Impact assessment, reporting

## Decision Support Needs
- Identify children needing immediate attention
- Optimize staff allocation based on workload
- Monitor service delivery gaps
- Track child development progress
- Forecast resource requirements

## Reporting Frequency
- **Daily:** Service alerts, new registrations
- **Weekly:** Progress reports, staff workload
- **Monthly:** Comprehensive analytics, KPI tracking
- **Quarterly:** Trend analysis, strategic planning

## Data Sources
1. Children demographic data
2. Service delivery records
3. Education progress data
4. Staff assignments and performance
5. Audit logs for compliance
